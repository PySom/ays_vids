    Documentation and Usage

    Frequently asked questions

    How can I personalize my app?
    From the sidebar, you can add/edit your image by tapping the camera icon and selecting the image of your choice.

    In addition, you can add your name to the app, simply tap on the "pen-book" icon. Fill in your name and select "save". Tap on save without writing anything if you want to cancel from typing.

    How do I watch a video?
    From the sidebar, select the video of your choice or search from the search bar.
    How do I learn from questions?
    Select the course of your choice from the side bar and start learning.
    My course says 404 error!
    That's embaracing, pull the application to refresh again. It should be fine
    The application cannot start
    Restart the application. If it is solved, happy learning.

    However, locate the app "KWS" on your device. Tap on the "globe" icon to restart the app engine

    I want to take a break, can I know where I left off?
    Yes you can. As long as you are on the application, the folder of the video you currently watched will be highlighted in white while the last video you watched will be highlighted in blue.

    The study pack retains your last course even after you close the application.

    What are the modes in the Study Pack?
    There are three (3) modes.
    Learn mode
    In this mode, you get to answer the questions and get the correct answer within two (2) trials. You will be able to see an interactive tutorial of each question covered.
    Time mode
    This simulates a standard examination condition where you examine at your own pace. You can always set the timer for each study pack.
    Freeform mode
    This is similar to the time mode only without the timer.
    The videos keep restarting, what do I do.
    Not your fault dear. Kindly watch the video in normal mode and avoid full screen view
    I cannot see any app icon to launch the app
    Kindly open chrome browser and type the url "localhost:8080" or "0.0.0.0:8080".
    If the app does not come on, open "KWS Pro" app on your device and tap the "globe" icon to restart the app.
    Golden Rule
    When the application malfunctions, restart "KWS PRO" app from the device.

    To do this, locate the app icon on the device called "KWS PRO". Click to launch. Tap on the globe icon to stop the app and tap again to start.
